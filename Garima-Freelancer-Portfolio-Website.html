FAQ

Common questions

Why hire a beginner freelancer instead of an agency?
Lower overhead means lower prices, and you get direct access to the person doing the work — no account manager, no handoffs, no waiting for a team's schedule to line up.
What file formats do you need from me?
Excel (.xlsx), CSV, or Google Sheets exports all work fine. If your data lives somewhere else, tell me and I'll let you know if I can work with it.
How many revisions are included?
One full round of revisions is included in the dashboard price. Additional changes after that are billed hourly, agreed in advance.
Do you sign an NDA?
Yes — happy to sign one before you share any data, no extra charge.

Have a dataset that needs cleaning up?

Send it over and I'll reply with a quick scope and timeline — usually within a day.
